Description

Ranch Chicken Tacos are a delicious and flavorful twist on traditional tacos, combining the tangy and creamy flavors of ranch dressing with tender and seasoned chicken. These tacos typically start with marinated and grilled chicken breasts, which are seasoned with a blend of spices such as garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and cumin. The chicken is cooked until juicy and tender, then thinly sliced or shredded to fill the tortillas.

The star ingredient of Ranch Chicken Tacos is the ranch dressing, which adds a creamy and tangy element to the dish. Ranch dressing is typically made with a combination of mayonnaise, sour cream, buttermilk, and a blend of herbs and spices like dill, parsley, and garlic. This flavorful dressing is drizzled over the chicken and acts as a creamy and cooling counterpart to the savory and seasoned chicken. To complete the tacos, additional toppings such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocados, and grated cheese can be added for extra texture and flavor. The combination of the zesty ranch dressing, seasoned chicken, and fresh toppings creates a delicious and satisfying taco experience that is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup ranch dressing
  • ¼ cup reduced-fat sour cream
  • 1 (1 ounce) packet taco seasoning mix, divided
  • 1 tablespoon mild chunky salsa
  • 2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 8 (6 inch) corn tortillas
  • shredded lettuce
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 1 (4 ounce) can sliced black olives
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and diced (Optional)
  • 1 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Combine ranch dressing, sour cream, 1 teaspoon taco seasoning, and salsa in a small bowl. Cover and refrigerate until serving.

  2. Toss chicken with remaining taco seasoning. Cover bowl loosely with wax paper or plastic wrap. Microwave chicken until chicken is heated through, about 2 to 3 minutes.

  3. Warm tortillas in a skillet for about a minute on each side to make them pliable. Place a scoop of chicken on the tortilla and top with lettuce, tomato, green onion, olives, avocado, cheese, and a spoonful of the ranch dressing mixture.
